Druxt.js Router

Simple decoupled Drupal routing for Nuxt.js

Get started

The Druxt.js Router provides a wilcard route, with it's Vuex powered JSON:API client, to bring simple decoupled Drupal routing to your Nuxt.js progressive web apps.
